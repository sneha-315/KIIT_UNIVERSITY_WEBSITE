Our Journey of Excellence

From humble beginnings to becoming an educational powerhouse

The KIIT Saga

1992

The Genesis

KIIT began as an Industrial Training Institute with just 12 students and 2 teachers. The vision of Prof. Achyuta Samanta to provide quality education to underprivileged children laid the foundation for what would become one of India's premier universities.

1997

Expansion to Engineering Education

Established as KIIT College of Engineering, offering B.Tech programs in Computer Science, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering. The institute quickly gained recognition for its industry-oriented curriculum and excellent placement record.

2004

Deemed University Status

Granted Deemed University status by the University Grants Commission (UGC), Ministry of HRD, Government of India. This marked a significant milestone, allowing KIIT to design its own curriculum and award degrees.

2017

Category-I University Status

Awarded Category-I University status by UGC, recognizing KIIT's excellence in teaching, research, and infrastructure. The university expanded to include Medical, Dental, Law, and Management schools.

2023

Global Recognition

Achieved NAAC A++ grade (3.61/4.0) - the highest accreditation grade in India. Ranked among top 20 Indian universities and featured in QS World University Rankings.

About KIIT University

A center of excellence in education, research, innovation, and social transformation

University Overview

KIIT University, Bhubaneswar stands as a shining beacon of higher education in India, consistently ranked among the top 20 universities in the country. Spread across a sprawling 400+ acre campus, the institution has transformed from a humble vocational training center in 1992 into a globally recognized multidisciplinary university that nurtures 30,000+ students from 65+ countries.

With its NAAC A++ grade (3.61/4.0) — the highest accreditation in India — and featured in QS World University Rankings, KIIT has established itself as a premier destination for students seeking world-class education, cutting-edge research opportunities, and holistic personality development. The university's vision, "To become a global leader in education, research, and innovation, creating responsible citizens and future leaders," drives every aspect of its academic and administrative endeavors.

Our Vision
